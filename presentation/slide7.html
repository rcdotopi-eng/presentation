Step 3: Matching & Fare Agreement

1. Driver Marks "Available"

After reviewing the distance and route, interested drivers hit the "Available" button to bid for the ride.

2. Interactive Rider Map

The rider now sees all available drivers populated on their live map. They can interact directly with the drivers through the app.

3. Mutually Settled Fare & Completion

The rider and driver mutually agree on a fair price. Once accepted, the driver arrives, the ride is completed, and the rider securely rates the driver.
